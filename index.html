basic_synth

this basic synthesizer was made with jquery and tone.js library - it was assembled as a final project for coderslab bootcamp that i've attended. you can use the synth either by clicking on the keys in the lower part of the UI, or by pressing the keys on your keyboard, corresponding to the labels in the UI. Z and X keys switch the current octaves. the synth controls are tweaked with your mouse.

the upper-left series of switches change the presets of the oscillators - including basic waveshape and ADSR envelope. the sliders underneath them adjust the duration of the notes triggered with your keyboard, and autofilter frequencies, respectively.

you can use the two big knobs in the middle of the UI by hovering over them with your mouse and using your scroll wheel. these will change your chorus and reverb settings, accordingly. and finally, the one last slider underneath them is basically your low-pass filter frequency control.

the speaker on the right is just a picture, it doesn't make any sound, dummy.
